Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my fav food. Here is a receipe for deliciously rich scrambles eggs.

Ingrdients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp cream

METHOD

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the cream and egg in a bowl.
  3. Once butter is melted, add eggs and cream in it.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edges of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omellete .
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate untill served .
